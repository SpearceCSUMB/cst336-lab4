Welcome Mercury!



The first planet in our solar systems, named after the messenger of the Roman gods, aka Hermes in Greek mythology. Being that Mercury is closest to the sun the planets orbit is very fast. A year in Mercury is 88 days, compared to our 365 (damn thats fast!). However, due to being almost completely gravitationally locked to the sun a day in Mercury lasts forever! 176 Earth days to one day in Mercury. Talk about the longest day of the year!

Even though Mercury is the closest planet to the sun it is considered the second hottest planet in the solar system. The first is Venus, even though Venus is second on the list, it has an atmosphere while Mercury has a very thin atmosphere because of how close the planet is to the sun, the solar winds are constantly blowing the atmoshpere away. Mercury reaches temerpatures as low as -280℉ and temperatures as high as 800℉! Crazy!
